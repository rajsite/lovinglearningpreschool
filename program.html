--- layout: default title: Parmer Lane Preschool: Program headerstuff: > scroller: Program: Learn about the activites your child can participate in and the goals we establish at the preschool --- At Parmer Lane Preschool, we emphasize individualized learning. Specialized activities and curriculum are created to better suit your child's needs and developmental stages. Our teachers work to assess your child's progress, their successes and difficulties, and personally focus on your child's development.

Toddlers (18 months to 2 and 1/2 years ):
Toddlers use a variety of materials specially designed for developing language, fine and gross motor skills, independence and sensory awareness, as well as a perception for music and the arts. Regular interaction with other children, our loving staff, and educational programs allow for the comprehensive development of basic verbal and social skills.

Pre-School (2 and 1/2 years to 5 years):
Children have the freedom to explore different activities and games, helping them develop independence, self-discipline, and strong work habits. Our curriculum has practical, sensory and life oriented elements as well as a focus on math, language, botany, geography, science, art, music appreciation, sign language, computer literacy and much more.
