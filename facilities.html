Facilities: Take a look at the facilities we have available

At Parmer Lane Preschool we make sure that each student is equipped with the proper facilities and materials to enhance their growth and ensure their development. Our three classrooms are equipped with books, educational supplies, games, and numerous activities and learning materials to suit your child’s needs.

Our sanitary kitchen area is there to make sure that your child will get to enjoy tasty, healthy, nutritious and freshly-cooked meals everyday. We will work to accommodate you with any special dietary needs.

Our outdoor area has plenty of space for children to play games and enjoy physical exercise. A full playscape and many outdoor toys are there to let your children have fun while at the same time maintaining their physical health.
