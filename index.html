Mission Statement:

Our mission is to help the children of today grow into the responsible, caring and successful individuals of tomorrow.

Background:

Loving Learning Preschool is a family owned and state-licensed child care home that has been running in Austin, Texas for over 10 years. The director of Loving Learning Preschool, Mrs. Reema Raj, has over 20 years of child care experience from teaching, substitute teaching, and raising two children of her own.

We are also very excited to have Ms. Shaheen Bano join us to help with teaching our students. Ms. Bano is a Montessori trained teacher with 5 years of experience working in preschool programs.

Join Mrs. Reema in her newest achievement, creating a preschool that helps to foster creativity in her students as well as provide a safe and encouraging learning environment where parents can rest assured that their children will have the opportunity to flourish.

We offer to all of our students:

  • A Preschool curriculum for children of ages 2 to 5 years
  • A safe outdoor play area and playscape
  • Individualized attention for each child
  • An introduction to basic Spanish and American Sign Language
  • Teachers who speak English, Hindi, Mahrathi, and Gujurathi
  • Meals at no extra cost and vegetarian meal options
